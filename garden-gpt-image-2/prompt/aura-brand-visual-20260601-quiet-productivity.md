# Aura Brand Visual Prompt

Create a quiet, low-distraction brand visual for "Aura", a Windows desktop AI companion for focus sessions, Pomodoro timing, local activity insight, daily reports, and a small optional desktop pet.

Format:
- 16:9 landscape image, suitable as a subtle app hero or empty-state background.
- No visible UI text, no fake screenshots, no readable words, no logos copied from real brands.
- Leave generous quiet space in the center-left for real UI overlay.

Visual direction:
- Mature desktop productivity tool, calm and precise, not a marketing landing page.
- A softly lit workbench-like abstract scene: layered translucent panels, faint time rings, tiny activity pulses, and a small companion presence suggested only as a warm rounded silhouette or soft light point.
- Color palette must match Aura's design system: warm off-white, muted paper green, deep ink, moss green accent, tiny restrained tomato-red accent.
- Use fine hairline details, soft matte materials, shallow depth, very subtle grain, and restrained contrast.

Composition:
- Background: quiet off-white to pale green-grey gradient with barely visible paper texture.
- Right side: abstract stacked glass-like rectangles and thin chart-like traces suggesting focus analytics.
- Lower-right corner: a tiny non-character companion glow, friendly but minimal.
- Avoid heavy gradients, purple-blue cyber style, neon, bokeh orbs, large decorative blobs, cartoon animals, busy dashboards, fake data, and oversized text.

Mood:
- Calm, trustworthy, private, local-first, long-session friendly.
- Designed for someone who will look at the app every day without visual fatigue.

Quality:
- Premium editorial product illustration, highly polished, subtle, realistic material lighting, no clutter.
